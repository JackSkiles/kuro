Pour Over

The keys to perfect pour over are patience and consistency. First we weigh the beans, ensuring the perfect coffee to water ratio each brew. We then grind the coffee beans to a medium-course consistency, allowing just enough of the flavors and aromas to be released from the beans. To begin the brew, we prime the beans with a small amount of water. This starts the blooming process, in which the hot water force the C02 gasses in the beans to be released, causing the grounds to bubble up and release beautiful aromas. After the bloom, we finish adding the water slowly, ensuring the brew does not finish too early.
